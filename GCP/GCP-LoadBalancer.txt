Two Types of Load Balancing
1. Global Loadbalancing
2.Regional Load balancing

Global LoadBalacing: Backend end points leaves in multiple region

Regional Load balancing: Backend end points leaves in a single region

Another type of load balancing
1.External LoadBalancing : Traffic coming from outside /from internet
2.internal LoadBalancing: Traffic shared within the GCP network

External LoadBalancing(Global): includes TCP, HTTP(s), SSL PROXY
Internal LoadBalancing(Regional): includes TCP and UDP traffic
