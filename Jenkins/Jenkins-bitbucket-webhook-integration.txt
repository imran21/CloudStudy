Bitbucket jenkins webhooks

Prerequsite on Jenkins
1. Bitbucket plugin should be installed in jenkins




On Bitbucket
Step 1: navigate to repo
Step 2: Click on repository settings
Step 3: Click on webhooks
Step 4: Provide the following details 
		Title - select any name
		URL - Jenkins URL Eg; https://cicd.apptium.com/bitbucket-hook/
		Note: "bitbucket-hook" after URL is needed
		Status -Enable Active
		SSL/TSL - Disable skip certificate verification
		Triggers - Select Push
		SAVE

On Jenkins
Step 1: Navigate to the project
Step 2: Navigat to configure page of the job
Step 3: Under "Build Triggers"
		Select - Build when a change is pushed to BitBucket
